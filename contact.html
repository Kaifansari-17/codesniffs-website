Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical response time?

We pride ourselves on quick response times. Sales inquiries typically receive a response within 2 hours during business hours. Technical support tickets are addressed within 4 hours for critical issues and 24 hours for non-critical issues. For urgent matters, our 24/7 phone support is always available.

Do you offer custom development solutions?

Yes! While we have ready-made templates for common business types, we specialize in custom development. Our team will work with you to understand your unique requirements and build a solution that perfectly fits your business needs, whether it's a slight modification to an existing template or a completely custom system from scratch.

What are your payment terms and methods?

We offer flexible payment options to suit your needs. Typically, we work with 50% advance payment and 50% upon delivery. We accept UPI, Net Banking, Credit/Debit Cards, and bank transfers. EMI options are available for larger projects. All payments are secured and GST invoices are provided for Indian clients.

What is included in your support package?

Our support packages include: bug fixes, minor updates, security patches, email support, and phone support during business hours. We also offer training sessions for your staff. Extended support packages are available for 24/7 coverage, priority response, and additional features. The exact details depend on the plan you choose.

Can you integrate with our existing systems?

Absolutely! We have extensive experience in API integrations. We can integrate with popular accounting software (Tally, QuickBooks), payment gateways (Razorpay, Paytm), CRM systems, inventory management software, and custom legacy systems. During the discovery phase, we'll assess your current systems and provide integration options.
